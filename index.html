‚õµ What is OpenShips?

Centuries ago, having your own ship gave you freedom to go anywhere you wanted. Instead of scrubbing decks, you stand proudly at the till charting your own course.

Web3 is giving us similar freedom to leave centralized web2 platforms behind and explore better alternatives in a new internet. We decided to have a little bit of fun along the way.

OpenShips is a project by adventure-loving degens who thirst for decentralization, freedom, frothy beverages and fighting for $SOS bullion.

We are building a community of like-minded folks who are ready for some web3 adventures, story-telling, and even the odd skirmish.

Why $SOS?

The enthusiasm around the $SOS airdrop showed there's a demand for decentralization in the NFT space.

But decentralization needs more than just tokens to happen. It needs places and reasons to put those tokens to work. Places that need to be built.

We are launching OpenShips -- with $SOS minting only -- to extend the value of your tokens beyond simply swapping them.
