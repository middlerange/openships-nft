Did You Say Racing!?

Hell yes we did. A large part of this project has been focused on being an early $SOS project and the first $SOS game.

After the artwork and attributes were sorted, we focused on creating the mechanics of the race and the race experience.

Each race is a unique combination of weather, catastrophes, good fortune, and distance to give different ship owners chances to win.

With the game taking shape, we returned to the ships to add utility. Now some OpenShips are better prepared for the rough seas while others are built for speed, not endurance. Just saying, you might want to grow your fleet on the secondary market.

At its core, $SOS was created to be a utility token for NFT marketplaces.
