What is OpenShips?

Centuries ago, having your own ship gave you freedom to go anywhere you wanted. Instead of scrubbing decks, you stand proudly at the till charting your own course.

Web3 is giving us similar freedom to leave centralized web2 platforms behind and explore better alternatives in a new internet.

OpenShips is a project by adventure-loving degens who thirst for decentralization, freedom, frothy beverages and fighting for $SOS bullion.
